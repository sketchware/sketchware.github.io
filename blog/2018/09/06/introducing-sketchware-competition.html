Introducing Sketchware Competition

September 6, 2018

Sung Woo Park

Hello, Sketchware users!

We're opening a very first Sketchware Competition.

Sketchware competitions will be held for a certain duration of time, depending on the type of the competition.

Winners will be selected based on the user interface and user experience of the application and the style of block programming.

After the competition ends, the winner will be selected within a few days, and he or she will be notified via email.

If you submit a project, you will be rewarded 100 points. If you win the competition, you will be rewarded 200 points and a $3 Google Play Gift card.

These points will determine your rank, and you will be eligible to enter different competitions at a higher rank.

For our first competition, please let your imaginations go wild and create a creative To-do list app!

If you need a good starting point for creating a To-do list app, you can refer to the Tutorial Lesson 2 under the Tutorials tab.
